Our Story

Where Style Meets Comfort

DecoWindow was born from a simple belief — every window deserves to tell a story. What started as a small family workshop in Mumbai has grown into a trusted name in window decor across India.

We work with master craftsmen and the finest textile mills to bring you curtains and curtain rods that combine timeless elegance with modern durability. Every piece is curated with care.
